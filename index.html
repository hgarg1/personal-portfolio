A few principles that show up i
  • Opinionated > generic. Good products say "this is the way" and then hold your hand through it.
  • Latent complexity, obvious surfaces. Advanced systems should feel simple until you need the power.
  • Everything is documentation. Naming, layout, commit messages—they all teach the next person how to extend the system.
  • No orphan features. If we can't explain what a feature is for, we either cut it or redesign it.
